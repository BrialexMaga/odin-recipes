Sandwich

Description

A very common recipe between girls which are not in the kitchen

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Ham
  • Cream
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Spread the cream on both slices of bread
  2. Put a slice of ham on one of them
  3. Put the lettuce on top of the ham
  4. Slice some tomatoes and put them on the lettuce
  5. Close the Sandwich with the other slice of bread
